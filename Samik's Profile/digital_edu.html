Insights on Computer Science, Digital Entrepreneurship, Education & Content Creation

Posted on July 17, 2025 by Samuel Kehem Iorlaha

My Passion for Computer Science

Computer science is more than just a field of study for me—it's a way of thinking and solving problems. From my early days as a student, I was fascinated by how algorithms and code could transform ideas into real-world solutions. Over the years, I have explored various programming languages, built web applications, and mentored students to develop their own tech skills. I believe that computer science is the backbone of innovation in today's digital world, and I am committed to making it accessible and exciting for everyone.

Embracing Digital Entrepreneurship

The digital age has opened up endless opportunities for entrepreneurship. My journey as a digital entrepreneur began with a simple desire to provide value and solve everyday problems using technology. Through ventures like Samik VTU and Easzy Data Sam, I have learned the importance of adaptability, customer service, and continuous learning. Digital entrepreneurship is not just about making money—it's about creating impact, building relationships, and staying ahead in a rapidly changing world.

Education: Teaching and Learning

As a Lecturer II at Harry Pass Polytechnic, Mkar-Gboko, I have the privilege of shaping the next generation of tech leaders. Teaching is a two-way street: while I share knowledge with my students, I also learn from their curiosity and fresh perspectives. I am passionate about using technology to enhance education, making learning interactive, practical, and relevant to real-life challenges. My goal is to inspire students to think critically, embrace innovation, and pursue lifelong learning.

My Journey as a Content Creator

Content creation has allowed me to reach a wider audience and share my expertise beyond the classroom. Through my YouTube channel, Samik Digital Lectures, and other social platforms, I create tutorials, share industry insights, and motivate aspiring tech enthusiasts. The journey hasn't always been easy—there are challenges like staying consistent, keeping up with trends, and engaging viewers—but the rewards are immense. Every message from a viewer who learned something new or was inspired to start their own journey makes it worthwhile.

Lessons Learned and Advice

  • Stay Curious: Technology evolves rapidly. Keep learning and experimenting.
  • Be Resilient: Both tech and entrepreneurship come with setbacks. Learn from failures and keep moving forward.
  • Share Knowledge: Teaching and content creation multiply your impact and help you grow.
  • Build Community: Surround yourself with like-minded people who support and challenge you.
  • Give Back: Use your skills and platforms to help others grow and succeed.

Final Thoughts

My journey in computer science, digital entrepreneurship, education, and content creation is ongoing. I am grateful for every opportunity to learn, teach, and inspire. If you are passionate about technology or want to start your own digital journey, remember: the best time to begin is now. Stay connected, keep learning, and never stop creating!

Have questions or want to collaborate? Contact me or connect with me on YouTube, Twitter, or LinkedIn.
